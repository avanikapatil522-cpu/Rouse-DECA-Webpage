About Our Chapter

Rouse DECA is an award winning organization dedicated to developing the next generation of leaders in marketing, business, finance, hospitality, and entrepreneurship. Through competition, workshops, travel, leadership training, and real world business experience, our members gain the confidence and skills needed to succeed in any professional environment.


Our chapter emphasizes collaboration, innovation, and personal growth. Whether you're preparing for competitive events, taking on leadership roles, or engaging in community projects, Rouse DECA provides the support and resources students need to excel.

Our Mission

Leadership Development

Students gain experience in project management, marketing strategy, finance, hospitality, and entrepreneurship.
